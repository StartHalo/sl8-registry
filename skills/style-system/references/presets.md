# Preset library — video-studio

Each preset: the six tokens + the full aesthetic block (prompt-facing text) + a "For:" fit
line. Derived 2026-07-22 from the skill-ecosystems study (vox-director's theme axes,
re-expressed and re-worded for our models) plus our own R02-proven block. The library is a
quality floor — compose new presets from `axes.md` when none fits.

## editorial-collage  ✅ proven on our models (R02, 2026-07-22)

- palette: "warm cream, ink black, one warm red accent"
- type_style: "bold condensed sans-serif capitals"
- finish: "halftone print grain, cut-paper archival texture, museum-print margins"
- mood: "editorial, evidentiary, confident"
- motion_style: punchy

**Aesthetic block:**
> Editorial collage, flat 2D composition on warm cream paper with visible halftone print
> grain and cut-paper archival texture. Restrained palette: cream ground, ink black, one
> warm red accent. Layered hand-cut paper elements with soft real drop shadows; hand-drawn
> accents and arrows. Bold condensed sans-serif capitals for any headline. Clean margins,
> museum-print finish. Printed / illustrated cut-outs, NOT CGI, NOT a 3D render — keep
> print grain and paper imperfections.

**For:** explainers, evidence-led editorial pieces, concept breakdowns. The default.

## newsprint-editorial

- palette: "cream white, deep red, mustard yellow, charcoal black"
- type_style: "bold condensed newsprint headlines, all-caps"
- finish: "aged paper, heavy halftone dots, high contrast, slight print misregistration"
- mood: "energetic, tactile, cinematic editorial"
- motion_style: punchy

**Aesthetic block:**
> Newsprint editorial collage, flat 2D layered paper cut-outs with torn and scissor-cut
> edges, tape corners and soft paper drop shadows on a bold flat colored paper background.
> Heavy halftone dots, newspaper-clipping scraps, aged paper texture, slight print
> misregistration, scattered geometric paper accents. Palette: cream white, deep red,
> mustard yellow, charcoal black. Bold condensed all-caps newsprint headline type.
> Printed cut-outs, not photoreal, no CGI, no 3D render.

**For:** news-style explainers, history/timeline pieces, high-energy topical videos.

## swiss-minimal

- palette: "white ground, near-black ink, one red accent"
- type_style: "grotesque sans (Helvetica-class), tight all-caps, strong grid"
- finish: "clean flat print, very subtle paper grain"
- mood: "precise, confident, calm"
- motion_style: calm

**Aesthetic block:**
> Swiss modernist poster design, flat 2D, strict typographic grid on a white ground.
> Near-black grotesque sans-serif type set tight, one red accent element, generous
> negative space, hairline rules. Clean flat print finish with very subtle paper grain.
> No gradients, no 3D, no photorealism, no decorative clutter.

**For:** product/tech explainers, data-led pieces, B2B clarity.
